# Research Method

## Objective

Build a high-signal, traceable corpus on organic LinkedIn strategy for B2B SaaS.

## Expert selection criteria

Each selected expert must meet most of the following criteria:

1. Direct operating experience in B2B SaaS, growth, demand generation, content, or category creation.
2. A documented body of public work with practical insights.
3. Recent activity on LinkedIn, YouTube, podcasts, or equivalent primary channels.
4. Clear relevance to buyer education, demand creation, distribution, positioning, or founder-led content.
5. Evidence of practicing the ideas they teach.

Audience size alone is not a selection criterion.

## Collection rules

### LinkedIn

LinkedIn posts are collected manually. No scraping or automated account activity is used.

Each captured post must include:

- canonical source URL;
- visible publication date, when available;
- collection date;
- post format;
- themes;
- a short researcher note explaining why it matters.

### YouTube

Transcripts are collected through a transcript API when available.

Each transcript must preserve:

- video title;
- video URL;
- publication date, when available;
- collection date;
- transcript provider;
- language;
- timestamps, when returned by the provider.

## Evidence standard

The project distinguishes between:

- **Observed evidence:** what a source explicitly states or demonstrates.
- **Recurring pattern:** a theme observed across multiple independent sources.
- **Future hypothesis:** an interpretation that could inform a future 100Hires content experiment.

Hypotheses are not represented as proven facts.
